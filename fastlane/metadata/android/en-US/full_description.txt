Kantine - Your digital menu for the Bessa canteen.

Kantine keeps you informed about the weekly menu offerings. The app displays menus for this week and next week in a clear list view.

Features:
- Login with your Bessa account
- Weekly view: This Week & Next Week
- Automatic DE/EN language detection via Splitter
- Modern Material 3 design with dynamic colors

Privacy & Security:
No registration required - simply log in with your Bessa account and start browsing. The app does not collect personal data, shows no ads, and does no tracking. All data is transmitted securely (HTTPS TLS 1.3).

Kantine only reads menu data - there is no ordering feature, no order history, and no push notifications.
